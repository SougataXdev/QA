Institutionalised Medical Excellence
We have established a professional and qualified medical structure that prioritises the strength of the institution over individual profiles. With an average doctor age of 38 years and a centre head attrition rate of approximately 2%, we maintain continuity, stability and deep institutional expertise. The long and fulfilling careers of many senior doctors and embryologists reflect the strong loyalty and engagement promoted within our organisation.
Continuous Professional Growth
We prioritise continuous learning to ensure our doctors, technicians, embryologists and care coordinators stay at the forefront of fertility science. Our professionals gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge technology, collaborate closely with R&D teams and develop expertise through managing some of the most complex infertility cases in the country.
Differentiated Talent Development
The Indira Fertility Academy acts as a specialised training hub, attracting and nurturing fresh postgraduates and young doctors. Through a targeted recruitment approach and early exposure to high case volumes and complex treatments, we accelerate clinical expertise development. Our substantial investment in training and education ensures our teams remain at the forefront of advanced technologies, industry best practices and research-driven innovation.
Performance-linked Rewards and Ownership Mindset
Our compensation structure promotes accountability and an ownership mindset. Centre heads receive a combination of fixed salary and revenue share, effectively managing doctor costs at 60–70% of the industry median while aligning individual performance with the organisation’s broader goals.
A Great Place to Work
Our people-first culture is reflected in an 83% employee engagement score and recognition as a Certified Great Place to Work. Through digital transformation in HR, we continue to enhance employee experiences, improve operational efficiency and support our teams in delivering their best.
